## Revision-4 Target

- Repository-shipped `synthesizeGoal` turns one IDE message into `goal-synthesis.md`, selects only approved catalog modules, compares serial and legal specialist-team candidates, and maps novel/high-risk owner decisions to the launched fields that reflect them.
- One `runGoal` facade covers start, identified/unidentified start rejection, and status-specific self-contained continuations with explicit legal interactions; requested concurrency defaults to one and is explicit above one.
- A closed PolicyBundle binds exact Circuit, Module, and WorkPacket templates; execution planning cannot invent policy.
- Semantic planning supplies one task-shaped AgentBlueprintIntent; independent pure `compileAgentBlueprints` accepts no runtime supply, derives EvidenceBindings first, and compiles one complete AgentBlueprint per invocation with exact objective, ports, registered context uses/field digests, scope, TaskAuthorityProjection, evidence, verification, handoff, and stop conditions.
- AgentProfile is runtime capability supply; fixed AgentBlueprint demand excludes that supply; Assignment is their sole binding; AgentMaterializationReceipt records the bounded host configuration claim before dispatch.
- GoalContract coverage compiles to qualified EvidenceBindings and core-owned EvidenceSatisfactions.
- Deterministic bounded conflict-safe waves, exact selected-wave maximum-cardinality matching, reserves, attempt replay prevention, and installed claims control parallel work without claiming global conflict-packing optimality.
- The real one-shot V10 return is held in an opaque core capture; host-reported substitutes cannot become accepted results.
- Causal clarification, per-activation approval, route-first outcomes, declared exits, joins, diagnosis/fix, and exhaustive terminal closure are explicit.
- AcceptedWorkAccumulator preserves finalized semantics across a fresh process; RepositoryStateRequest binds the pre-request state; final RepositoryStateWitness, AcceptedWorkProjection, MemoryCandidates, and then MergeReadyEvidence are required in an acyclic completion order.
- Closed SourceReferences, one derived-digest registry, detached GrantOffer references, exact byte-reachable limits/comparators, and general portability preserve traceability without retaining prompts, hidden reasoning, or secrets.
