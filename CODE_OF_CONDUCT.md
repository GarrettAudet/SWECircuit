# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Participants should keep discussion respectful, specific, and evidence-oriented. Strong disagreement is welcome when it improves the work and stays focused on artifacts, decisions, risks, and outcomes.

Expected behavior includes:

- Assume good intent while still checking evidence.
- Be clear about tradeoffs and uncertainty.
- Keep criticism specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and maintainer decisions.
- Help make the project easier for humans and agents to use.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discriminatory language, public sharing of private information, deliberate disruption, bad-faith issue flooding, or attempts to bypass security and approval boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove disruptive content and may limit participation when behavior harms the project or community. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented when appropriate, and focused on restoring a useful working environment.
